Chameleon Hide — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026 · Publisher: Polyque Games

Chameleon Hide ("the Game") is a real-time multiplayer hide-and-seek game published by Polyque Games. This policy explains what data the Game processes.

Data We Collect

  • Nickname: the display name you enter is stored on your device and shared with other players in the same match. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Gameplay data: positions, paint strokes and round state are relayed between players in real time through Photon (Exit Games GmbH) servers and are not permanently stored.
  • Analytics & stability: we use Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics) to understand aggregate usage and fix crashes. This may process device identifiers, device model, OS version and coarse region.
  • Purchases: in-app purchases (if offered) are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We never see your payment details.

What We Do NOT Collect

No account or login is required. We do not collect your name, e-mail, contacts, photos, precise location, or any special-category data. The Game is not directed at children under 13 and contains no third-party behavioral advertising.

Third-Party Services

  • Photon Realtime / Fusion — privacy policy
  • Google Firebase — privacy policy
  • RevenueCat — privacy policy

Data Retention & Your Rights

Match data is transient. Analytics data is retained by Firebase per its standard retention settings. You may request deletion of analytics data tied to your device identifiers by contacting us.
